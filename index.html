Job packet to field packet, pricing, purchase order routing, and post-PO handoff.

IID employees upload the job packet. Automation extracts construction-map work, creates SharePoint records and folders, enriches the job with dig-ticket coordinates, creates a prefielding PDF, supports tasking/pricing, and routes purchase-order PDFs into the correct job folder.
